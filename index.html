Mi Maratha — Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your account

You can delete your Mi Maratha account and associated data in two ways:

Option 1: Delete from the app

  1. Open the Mi Maratha app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us with the email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

Request deletion via email
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent. All your data—including profile, posts, messages, matrimony profile, and uploaded media—will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Data that will be deleted

  • Profile information
  • Posts and comments
  • Chat history
  • Matrimony profile
  • Temple connections
  • B2C listings
  • Notifications
  • Uploaded media
